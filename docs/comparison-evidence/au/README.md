_Per-competitor evidence for `/trade-off/au/compare-platforms/`. Required before public publish per `docs/LEGAL_AU_COMPARATIVE_ADVERTISING.md`._

**Why this matters:** Post-November 2022 ACL amendments raised s.29 corporate penalties to the greatest of AUD $50M, 3× benefit, or 30% of adjusted turnover per contravention. AU is the highest-stakes jurisdiction on Earth for misleading comparative advertising. Contemporaneous evidence is the substantiation defence.

## AU-specific evidence rules

- **Pricing is highest-risk cell** — Hipages credit blocks + Airtasker tier commissions are dynamic. Screenshot the current pricing page + note the state/postcode filter used.
- **Dated screenshot required** for every price cell. PNG only.
- **Filename convention**: `pricing-{YYYY-MM-DD}.png`, `features-{YYYY-MM-DD}.png`.
- **Oneflare monitoring** — announced closure 30 Jun 2026. After that date, either remove the row or mark historic. Leaving stale = ACL s.18/s.29 breach.
- **GST inclusive vs exclusive** — AU pricing convention varies. Record whether the price shown is inc-GST or ex-GST. ServiceSeeking prices are inc-GST per their own docs; Airtasker commissions are ex-GST. Getting this wrong = misleading representation.
- **Legal hold** — if a competitor changes their page after you scored them, keep the old screenshot as proof of what was true when you scored.
